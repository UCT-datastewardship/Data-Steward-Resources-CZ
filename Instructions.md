### How to Contribute

To propose a new addition to the resources table, please navigate to **Issues** and open a new issue using the template:
> üìù [**Add New Entry to Database**](https://github.com/UCT-datastewardship/Data-Steward-Resources-CZ/issues/new/choose)

This template provides a structured form to collect all necessary information, allowing your submission to be processed efficiently.

---

### Submission Guidelines

* **Review Process:** To ensure all links are functional and information is accurately categorized, all submissions undergo an audit before being accepted and added to the list.
* **Form Completeness:** Please fill out as many fields in the form as possible; complete entries are much more likely to be approved quickly.
* **General Inquiries:** For communication, feedback, or other questions, please open a **separate issue** with your query.
* **Please make sure to add a full https:// link.**
---

### Contributor Engagement
If you are interested in a more active role or wish to be officially recognized as a project contributor, please get in touch. You can initiate this process by opening an issue to discuss potential areas for deeper collaboration.
